Pluritine Church

S1

The dominant religious institution of Dolmenwood, worshipping through an extensive canon of saints. The Church maintains a presence in Lankshorn through the Church of St. Pastery and its priest Father Dobey. The Bishop resides at Brackenwold, which serves as the seat of the Church in the region.

  • The Pluritine Church historically allied with the Drune and the nobility to hold the Cold Prince and his frost elves at bay. Those alliances have since faltered; the Drune are now widely regarded as heretical Hooded Men.
  • The Church of St. Pastery in Lankshorn is built from antique stone with a tall steeple and carved gargoyles and angels in conflict. Its interior is plain and unadorned. A wooden statue of St. Pastery — depicted holding a shovel and cleaver — stands inside.
  • Father Dobey serves as vicar of St. Pastery. He admitted two male breggles to the church late at night in Session 4; neither returned. Constable Wolder claims ignorance of this.
  • The Bishop resides at Brackenwold. Brother Henrick suspects the Bishop is responsible for Alfred's lycanthropy curse.
  • Toadstool spores near the Golokstone induced Pluritine hallucinations in the party — saints beckoning, sacred figures in religious iconography.
