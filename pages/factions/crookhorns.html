Crookhorns

S1

Corrupted breggles — one-horned, rabid, and dangerous. Created by the Nag-Lord, a feared figure in the region.

  • Four were discovered and killed at Droomen Knoll in Session 1: Berk, Drogba, and two unnamed. A fifth — the rabid crookhorn — fled down the stairs into the dungeon.
  • In Session 2, the rabid crookhorn and a sixth, one-horned crookhorn were killed in the storage room beneath Droomen Knoll.
  • The Constable and Burgomaster of Lankshorn offered a bounty for killing crookhorns. The bounty was paid out by Constable Sidney Wolder.
  • The Nag-Lord is known as the creator of crookhorns. A Gaff letter found at Droomen Knoll named Constable Sidney Wolder as a contact regarding crookhorn matters, suggesting Wolder may be in league with them.
  • Someone returned to the storage room after the party left and made precise puncture wounds in the breggle corpses.
