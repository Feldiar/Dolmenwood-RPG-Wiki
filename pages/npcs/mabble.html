Mabble

S1

Father Dobey's servant. Short, portly, and white-haired. Went missing and was found unconscious in the dungeon beneath Droomen Knoll.

  • Wooden prayer beads found on the Ditchway belonged to Mabble; Brother Henrick is holding onto them.
  • One of three people who went missing, linked to the strange pipe music.
  • Found unconscious in the dungeon beneath Droomen Knoll, held by the crookhorns. Had been following the sound of the pipe music, became enchanted, and was struck from behind.
  • Did not see or hear any other prisoners while drifting in and out of consciousness.
  • Rescued and returned to Father Dobey's cottage. Head wound dressed by Father Dobey.
