Sgt. Marge Bregson

S2

A breggle sergeant at the Lankshorn constabulary. The party sought her out after Constable Wolder proved unhelpful. She mentioned rumours of King Pusskin to the south.

  • No more willing than Wolder to send guards into Droomen Knoll.
  • After Jack's persistent appeal, agreed to send one soldier — Brennan — with the party as an informant, on the condition they do their best to protect him.
  • Mentioned rumours of a King Pusskin to the south.
  • Allegiance uncertain: may be a genuine ally, or another agent in the same web as Wolder.
