Princess Andromethia

S3

A fairy princess whose domain, the Blossom Fields, is populated by elves. Her lands of hills and meadows instill a feeling close to ecstasy. Lucien was drawn into her realm in Session 3 and remained five days.

  • Lucien was taken to her Blossom Fields in Session 3 and remained there for five days.
  • Holds lands in the mortal realm as well, somewhere in the south of Dolmenwood.
  • In Session 4, confirmed the location of the Naom waterfall (feeds into Lake Longmere) and noted a frost elf embassy stands nearby — key to Thinwhistle's riddle.
  • Extended an invitation to Lucien to return to the Blossom Fields.
  • Warned Lucien about her sister: a figure who rules the Deathglades from a palace of white marble, titled the Lady of Spring Unending.
  • Provided information on the fairy roads: the Moggle Door near Lankshorn runs north to Willowgate; Buttercup Lane connects the Rosy Gate (northeast) to the Emerald Door (northwest).
