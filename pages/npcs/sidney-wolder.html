Constable Sidney Wolder

S2

The constable of Lankshorn. Met the party in a back room at the constabulary, refilling his own drink and offering none to his guests. He paid out the crookhorn bounty and advised the party not to return to Droomen Knoll.

  • Paid out the crookhorn bounty to the party.
  • Advised the party not to return to Droomen Knoll.
  • Suggested the party speak with Sgt. Marge Bregson if they wanted support.
  • A Gaff letter found at Droomen Knoll named Wolder as a contact regarding crookhorn matters, suggesting he may be in league with them. Llerg translated the letter; the party bribed him to keep quiet.
  • In Session 4, lured into the woods by Brother Henrick under the pretense of investigating suspicious activity at the Church of St. Pastery. Ambushed and paralyzed by a divine miracle called down by Henrick. Bound and interrogated.
  • Confessed that he arranged for children to go missing from Lankshorn not on Nag-Lord orders, but to undermine Lord Malbleat and reinstate Lord Ramius. He made contact with Shub's Nana — a crone who serves the Nag-Lord independently — and used her silver goblin network to move the children north.
  • Constable speaking to silver goblins directly; no crookhorn involvement in the abductions.
  • A crystal vial of liquid with a nutty aroma was found on his person by Jack.
