Alfred

S5

A man in his mid-forties, dirty and wild-haired when first encountered, who had been suffering from lycanthropy. Cured by Brother Henrick, he woke with bluer eyes and a slightly younger face. He was cursed at Castle Brackenwold and seeks redemption.

  • Encountered in the woods at night, pursuing a unicorn he hoped would cure his lycanthropy.
  • The unicorn judged him unworthy of its blessing.
  • Tomkin encountered him first; Alfred attempted to bite Tomkin before being fended off. Tomkin offered him a place by the fire.
  • Brother Henrick cured his lycanthropy through divine intercession — Alfred needed only to ask.
  • Post-cure: bluer eyes, younger appearance, dense hair.
  • Came from Castle Brackenwold, where he was cursed. Henrick suspects the Bishop was responsible.
  • Chose to travel with the party, hoping for redemption.
  • Needs equipment — the party plans to outfit him in Dreg.
