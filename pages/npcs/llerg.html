Llerg

S1

A breggle hunter who frequents Hornstoat's Rest in Lankshorn. Sells meat to Bogways. Can read Gaff, the breggle language.

  • Filled the party in on recent disappearances: Emelda, Farnham Ribblemead, and Mabble.
  • Spoke of the strange pipe music drifting from the eastern woods.
  • Translated the Gaff letter found in Droomen Knoll, which revealed Constable Sidney Wolder to be a spy for the Nag-Lord. Was troubled by what it said.
  • Bribed with gold by the party to keep quiet about the letter. The party also attempted to charm him; it didn't take.
  • In Session 3, approached Jack and Henrick at Hornstoat's Rest seeking more bribe money. Had been accosted at the Bogways by a group of pro-Ramius breggles: Marialla, James, and Fisk. These three are almost certainly affiliated with the crookhorn conspiracy — and Session 4 confirmed they are the ones who deliver children to Shub's Nana's silver goblins.
  • Paid forty gold by the party to travel south to High Hankle and lie low for a few weeks. Mentioned a shrine to Pusskin on the road south — milk is the correct offering for those seeking its favor.
  • Noted that the Hlerribuck breggle festival falls on the fourth of Parchment.
