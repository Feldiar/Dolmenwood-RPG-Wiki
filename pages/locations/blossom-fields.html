Blossom Fields

S3

The fairy domain of Princess Andromethia, characterized by rolling farmland, hills, and meadows that instill a feeling close to ecstasy in visitors. Its inhabitants are half-elf and half-satyr.

  • Lucien was transported here in Session 3 and remained for five days, unable or unwilling to leave.
  • Princess Andromethia also holds lands in the mortal realm, somewhere in the south of Dolmenwood.
