Jaunty Horn

S4

A lively inn along the Ditchway, roughly eight and a half miles from Lankshorn — a half-day's walk. Run by Jezebel, a former adventurer. Hundreds of horseshoes cover the walls. Two children, siblings, are often underfoot.

  • The site where Constable Wolder was grabbed by silver goblins several months ago and taken to Shub's Nana's cottage, nearly a day's travel south.
  • Proprietor Jezebel bought the inn with adventuring earnings. She dreams of hunting a yellow bile wyrm south of Prigwort as her last great adventure.
  • Jezebel keeps a bottle of nippers in stock for grimalkin guests.
  • In Session 5, the Wooden Wolves arrived carrying their stricken companion Brehder, who had been drained of color and vitality by a mysterious stone.
