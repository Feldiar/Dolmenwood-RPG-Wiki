Man of Gold

S4

An apothecary tucked in a narrow alley near Hornstoat's squat building and its two-story tower in Lankshorn. Run by Sidewich Maldwort.

  • Stocks amber nectar (heals 1d4, 200gp — chance of inducing sleep), devil's grease (psychedelic fungal slime mold with ultraviolet aura, smells of strange sausage), and common Dolmenwood herbs.
  • Maldwort noted that spirithame (an herb) can be found a day's walk northwest of Lankshorn. Also mentioned Hay's Addle (20-mile herb-gathering stretch along Lake Longmere, hag warning) and blood canker near the Mossling domain.
