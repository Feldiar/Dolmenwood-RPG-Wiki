The Constabulary

S2

A wood and stone building in Lankshorn. A breggle clerk in red leather armor with a horned helmet mans the front desk.

  • Constable Sidney Wolder met the party here in a back room. He paid out the crookhorn bounty and advised them not to return to Droomen Knoll — then suggested they seek out Sgt. Marge Bregson if they wanted support.
  • The party sought official backing for their return to Droomen Knoll and were not taken seriously.
  • Sgt. Marge Bregson operates here; she reluctantly agreed to send Brennan with the party as an informant.
