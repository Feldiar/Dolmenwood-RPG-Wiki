Shub's Nana

Active S4

Spun off from The Crooked Constable. Constable Wolder's confession revealed that the children missing from Lankshorn were not taken by crookhorns but by a crone called Shub's Nana, who serves the Nag-Lord. She has been sending them north to be eaten. The party plans to pursue her south.

Objectives

  1. Locate Shub's Nana's cottage — brick, beside a dark rushing brook, nearly a day south of Lankshorn.
  2. Rescue the children she has taken.
  3. Stop the pipeline: the three breggles (Marialla, James, and Fisk) who deliver children to the silver goblins.

Leads & Clues

  • Constable Wolder was transported by silver goblins from near the Jaunty Horn inn along the Ditchway to the crone's cottage, then returned — suggesting the cottage is roughly a day south.
  • The phrase Leeleeglablea summons the silver goblins on a moonlit night.
  • The crone travels by sleigh.
  • The three pro-Ramius breggles — Marialla, James, and Fisk — are the ground-level operatives who bring children to the goblins. They were also the ones who accosted Llerg at the Bogways.
  • Shub's Nana is not a crookhorn but serves the Nag-Lord independently. See also: The Crooked Constable.
