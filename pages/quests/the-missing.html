The Missing

Active S1

Three people have disappeared from Lankshorn, possibly connected to strange pipe music heard in the eastern woods. The music is said to pull powerfully at the heart of anyone who hears it.

Objectives

  1. Find Emelda — missing for over a week. Known for her lovely singing voice; acted completely normally before vanishing.
  2. Find Farnham Ribblemead — a sage who disappeared after reading a mysterious letter and entering the woods.
  3. Find Mabble — RESOLVED. Father Dobey's servant was found unconscious in the dungeon beneath Droomen Knoll, held by crookhorns. He had followed the pipe music, been enchanted, and struck from behind. He was rescued and returned to Father Dobey's cottage.

Leads & Clues

  • Strange pipe music drifting from the eastern woods — people describe it as pulling at the heart.
  • The pipe music originated from Thinwhistle, imprisoned in the cells beneath Droomen Knoll. He has been freed.
  • Mabble's prayer beads were found on the Ditchway.
  • Mabble reported he did not see or hear any other prisoners while held captive.
  • Emelda and Farnham Ribblemead were definitively not among the prisoners at Droomen Knoll. Their whereabouts remain unknown.
  • Sidewich Maldwort of the Man of Gold knew Farnham Ribblemead and describes him as a linguist.
  • Father Dobey admitted two male breggles to the Church of St. Pastery late one night; neither returned. Connection to the missing persons unclear.
