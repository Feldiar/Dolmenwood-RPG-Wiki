Crookhorn Bounty

Closed S1

The Constable and Burgomaster of Lankshorn offered a bounty for killing crookhorns. The party collected the bounty in Session 2 — though Constable Wolder, who paid it out, may himself be in league with the crookhorns.

Objectives

  1. Kill crookhorns and report back to the Constable and Burgomaster — Completed. Bounty paid out by Constable Sidney Wolder without proof of kills.
  2. Six crookhorns were encountered in total: four killed at Droomen Knoll in Session 1, and the remaining two — including the rabid crookhorn that fled Session 1 — killed in the dungeon in Session 2.
